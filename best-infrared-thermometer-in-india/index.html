Best Infrared Thermometer in India(FAQ)

1. How to use an IR thermometer?

Using an IR thermometer is easier than you think. You’ve to just put the batteries and press the trigger button. After that, you’re ready to go. There are individual buttons for every function like object/body temperature measurement, C/F change, memory saving, or beep function. To measure temperatures, point the IR sensor to the object/forehead/ear within the required distance & press the trigger button.

2. How to set an IR thermometer?

Put new batteries into the IR thermometer. Then press the trigger button. The IR thermometer will blink up with green light(varies on different brands). After that, You can set the device to object/body mode or C/F mode by pressing the consecutive buttons.

3. How to reset an infrared thermometer?

You don’t have to reset the IR thermometers at all. You can simply measure new reading by pressing the trigger button again. It will overlap the old reading.

4. How to check fever with the digital thermometer?

Normally our body temperature remains 36,.9 degrees Celsius or 98.6 degrees Fahrenheit. If the IR thermometer readings show more temperature than the norm, then you must have a fever. However, The IR thermometer comes with a fever indicator feature. It indicates fever by changing the screen from green(normal) to orange(a bit high than normal) and red(fever). Apart from that, it also alerts you with a beep sound.

5. How to use IR thermometers on the forehead?

You can use an IR thermometer both on your ear and forehead. To use It your forehead, you’ve to aim your forehead with the IR sensor/nozzle of the device. After that, press the trigger button to measure the temperature of your forehead.

6. How accurate are readings on IR thermometers?

The accuracy of a thermometer depends on the emissivity. For an accurate IR thermometer, the sensor’s emissivity setting and the surface’s emissivity should match.

7. Can we use IR thermometer underarm?

No, you can’t use an IR thermometer on your underarms. You’re advised to use it on your ear or forehead.
