Things to consider before buying microfiber clothes â€“

Microfiber cloths are known for their cleaning efficiency. Yet, all of them are not. The efficiency depends on their GSM level and few other factors.

However, before moving to that part, you should know why microfiber cloths are more effective than regular clothes. It is mainly because of their composition.

The microfiber cloths are made of 80% polyester and 20% of polyamide. These microfibers are more efficient than cotton clothes as they have high absorbing power.

The sensitive strands of the microfiber towels can easily trap dirt and debris, making them really useful in cleaning surfaces. Besides, their softness gives you streak-free cleaning on glassing or sensitive surfaces.

However, their thickness or softness generally depends on the GSM level, as mentioned previously.

What is GSM?

GSM is known as grams per square meter (g/m2). It defines the thickness or weight of the material. In general, a higher GSM level means that the particular material is thicker and weighs more.

So, what is the difference between higher & lower GSM microfiber clothes?

Microfiber cloths having lower GSM levels come with a fewer number of piles. It means the clothes have fewer cotton layers lying inside them.

So, these kinds of microfiber cloths are used to be thin and lightweight. Also, they do have lower absorption power. Overall, the lower GSM microfiber cloths are less plush comparatively, not good for polishing or cleaning glasses.

On the other hand, the higher GSM level makes the cloth plusher. They do have better absorbing power and are super smooth & durable. You can quickly recognize these clothes by checking their weight and thickness.

For general cleaning, 300 GSM microfiber cloths are the best and economical choice. Let us distinguish the different usages of lower to higher GSM microfiber cloths.
