Different types of Massage Chairs\u200b:<\/h2>\n

1. Full Body Massage Chairs: <\/b><\/strong><\/h4>\n

A full-body massage chair is the most preferred choice for buyers despite being expensive. The full-body massage chairs do exactly what they are described. They do massage the whole body effectively and provide royal pleasure and stress relief at home.<\/p>\n

There are plenty of options available for a Full Body Massage Chair. <\/b><\/strong>But the quality, features, and performance may vary for different brands. However, All the full body massage chairs have features to massage your neck, body shoulders, calves, and feet. Also, the new models even can do hands, arms & hips.<\/p>\n

Moreover, the full-body massage chairs are completely adjustable. You can easily set them according to your requirement. Also, you can listen to music while enjoying a massage by connecting your smartphone to the chair.<\/p>\n

2. Shiatsu Massage Chairs: <\/b><\/strong><\/h4>\n

Shiatsu is a form of Japanese bodywork where fingers, thumb, and palms are used to apply deep pressure on your body. The shiatsu massage chairs also do the same thing artificially. Generally, the shiatsu massage chairs use kneading nodes to create deep pressure on your body and provide relaxation by applying a certain amount of heat.<\/p>\n

Shiatsu massage chairs come in a lot of varieties like Shiatsu Full body massage chair<\/b><\/strong>, Electric Heat Shiatsu Machine Body Massagers<\/b><\/strong> (for Cervical Neck Shoulder & Back Pain Relief), Deep Kneading Massage Seat Cushion Massage Chair<\/b><\/strong> and more. The price of the chairs varies from 5000rs to 2lacs rs.<\/p>\n

3. Recliner Massage Chairs:<\/b><\/strong><\/h4>\n

If you’re looking for a great flexible massage chair, then you should go for a recliner. It helps a lot to relax your back. Generally, the massage chairs come with a backrest. You can recline the backrest according to your comfort level. Even a footrest is available on Recliner Massage Chairs, <\/b><\/strong>which can be extended further to keep your leg linear.<\/p>\n

4. 2D\/3D\/4D Massage Chairs: <\/b><\/strong><\/h4>\n

You may get confused between 2D, 3D, or 4D massage chairs. Don’t worry, we have presented a simplified solution for this.<\/p>\n

2D Massage Chairs <\/b><\/strong>are the same as a 2D plane having an x-axis & y-axis. The roller of the traditional chairs moves up and down from your neck to the lower back. Also, the chairs can massage your body side by side. Moreover, you can adjust the movement speed to feel a smooth relaxation.<\/p>\n

3D Massage Chairs<\/b><\/strong> are also the replication of a three-dimension. They are a little bit expensive than the traditional 2D massage chairs with some additional features. Not only the massage chairs can move along with your body, but they can also press your back with decent pressure. It feels like having a real human massage.<\/p>\n

4D Massage Chairs<\/b><\/strong> Comes with 4D technology. The rollers of the chairs can turn, provide you the most effective massage. However, four dimensions don’t exist in reality. It’s a marketing strategy to sell 3D massage chairs by adding some additional speed and intensity to them.<\/p>\n

5. Zero Gravity Massage Chairs:<\/b><\/strong><\/h4>\n

The Zero Gravity massage chairs provide feelings like a space launch. Generally, the massage chairs recline your back at a certain position and elevate your feet & leg over your heart. This position evenly distributes your body weight along with the chair while massaging. So, it feels you’re on zero gravity. The Zero Gravity Massage Chairs help a lot to relieve stress from your spine.<\/p>\n
