Types of IP Cameras:<\/h3>\n

1. Fixed Position IP Cameras:<\/h4>\n

These types of IP cameras offer a wide-angle view with a decent digital zoom feature<\/strong>. But, the lens position of the camera is fixed. However, these cameras able to cover 100% area under its range. Fixed-position cameras can be of medium resolution(1MP)<\/strong> to higher resolution(2MP or 3MP).<\/strong> These cameras do not have a pan-tilt feature and significantly less expensive than PTZ cameras.<\/p>\n

2. PTZ IP Cameras:<\/h4>\n

The Pan-tilt-zoom(PTZ) cameras come with additional features to monitor a wide range view of your home. PTZ cameras allow the user to pan, tilt, or zoom with automatic or manual control<\/strong> based on their needs. A single PTZ camera can cover the whole area of your room. You don’t need to install different cameras for all angles.<\/p>\n

3. Wireless IP cameras:<\/h4>\n

Wireless cameras can be placed anywhere because there is no issue of wire in these cameras. You can simply connect these cameras wirelessly to any hub<\/strong>. So, the IP cameras are best for home surveillance. The IP cameras can be made wireless just by installing an additional antenna.<\/p>\n

4. Day\/Night IP cameras:<\/h4>\n

Such IP cameras can record views 24\/7 hours. Day\/Night cameras have IR LED bulbs<\/strong> installed within them. It allows capturing clear views even in the complete darkness<\/strong>. During the day, it offers full-color images. While at night, these cameras switch to Night Vision mode and capture views in B\/W mode only.<\/strong><\/p>\n

5. Indoor\/Outdoor IP cameras:<\/h4>\n

The best indoor security camera is specially designed for residential, office, and retail environments.<\/strong> These cameras offer several features like IR night vision, PTZ, motion detection. While the best outdoor security camera system is encased with IP66 or IP6-7 rated weatherproof<\/strong> housing that protects the camera from moisture, wind, dust, and other kinds of stuff<\/strong>. Also, many outdoor IP cameras offer day\/night feature for 24\/7 security.<\/p>\n
