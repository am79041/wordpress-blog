1. Glass Containers:<\/h3>\n

<\/p>\n

Glass containers are the most suitable material for kitchen storage. These jars not only preserve your cooking items but also enhance the charms of your kitchen. Also, for being transparent, it\u2019s always easier to differentiate between multiple cooking items.<\/p>\n

Inside the glass containers, you can store both dry (spices, dry fruits\/foods, cereals, wheat) and liquid items like oil, pickles, etc. Don’t worry about spilling, as the lids are 100% airtight. However, oil makes the glass surface slippery, so you have to be careful while picking them up.<\/p>\n

Moreover, you can microwave the glass jars comfortably. So, you can enjoy fresh dry food anytime by heating these containers a little.<\/p>\n
